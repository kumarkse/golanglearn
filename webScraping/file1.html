DAO Programs & Initiatives Entropy Advisors Updates
cliffton.eth August 26, 2024, 4:27pm 1

Entropy Advisors (@Entropy )is a firm focused on improving DAO governance, and is working exclusively with the Arbitrum DAO until [at earliest] September 2025 per the terms laid out in our previously passed onchain proposal. This channel can be used to get in touch with our team, and will also serve as the home for our quarterly transparency reports, monthly short-term deliverables updates, and other miscellaneous updates related to our work.
